FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How does your trip planning service work?

Our trip planning service uses AI to analyze your preferences, budget, and travel dates to create a personalized itinerary. You can specify your interests, accommodation preferences, and must-visit attractions, and our system will generate an optimized plan with real-time availability and pricing.

Is there a fee for trip modifications?

No, we don't charge any fee for making modifications to your trip plan. You can adjust your itinerary, change accommodations, or update activities as many times as you like before finalizing your booking.

What pricing plans do you offer?

We offer three pricing tiers: Basic (free), Premium ($9.99/month), and Elite ($19.99/month). The free plan includes basic trip planning, while premium plans offer features like real-time weather updates, 24/7 travel support, exclusive deals, and customized itineraries with local experiences.

Do you provide customer support?

Yes, we provide customer support via chat, email, and phone. Basic users can access email support with a 24-hour response time, while Premium and Elite users get priority support with faster response times and 24/7 emergency assistance during their trips.

What is your refund policy?

For subscription plans, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. For bookings made through our platform, refund policies vary by service provider. We clearly display the cancellation and refund terms before you complete your booking. In case of any issues, our customer support team will help you navigate the refund process.
